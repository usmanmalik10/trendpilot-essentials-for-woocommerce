== Description ==

**Trendpilot Essentials: Upgrade Your Woocommerce Store Today**

Enhance your store with essential optimization features designed to increase conversions, spotlight key products, and provide valuable insights into your key metrics.

**Maximize Conversions and Highlight Products**

If your store isn't utilizing these features, you're missing out on potential conversions. By highlighting key products, you can make the most of every visitor's experience.

**In-Depth Analytics for Informed Decisions**

Our detailed analytics section offers insights into your top-performing products and categories. This data empowers you to make informed decisions about which products to promote on the frontend.

**Core Product of the Trendpilot System**

Trendpilot Essentials is the foundation of our Trendpilot system. All features in the free plugin can be automated in our Pro product. Visit [trendpilot.io](https://trendpilot.io) to discover how we can further optimize your storefront through automation.

== Full Essentials Feature List ==

- **Analytics:** Analyse Product views, Category views, Upsell add-to-carts, Recommended Section clicks
- **Upsells:** Upsell a product pre-checkout via an upsell modal. View upsell add to cart data in Analytics
- **Recommended Section:** Add a new 'orderby' option to the Woocommerce shop page, 'Recommended'. Use this section to keep key products at the beginning of your shop.
- **Product Badges:** Add badges to your products in the shop loop and product page. Choose: Popular, New, Sale or custom text.
- **Top Bar:** Adds a top bar to your store page for you to add key messages to your visitors.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Use the Dashboard->Trendpilot Essentials for Woocommerce screen to configure the plugin.
